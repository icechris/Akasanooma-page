AKASANOMA

Akasanoma is a Machine Learning Local Language Translation Engine (MLLLTE) project. Akasanoma turns to bridge the gap between Technology and Language, ​giving a digital voice to the people and helping to preserve our language for our children and the ones after them.
Your voice is needed to make Akasanoma possible!

We at VersiTech  believe that this digital language divide can be brigded with these underrepresented languages at the trial generated electronically by the people for the people which is what we set out to do with the Akasanoma project.
